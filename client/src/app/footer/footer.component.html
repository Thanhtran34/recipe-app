Instructions for New User
  • You must provide unique username and email.
  • Your email must have format @xxx.com.
  • Your password must contain at least one special character like %, &, @, ?, $, #...
  • Use AddRecipe to create a new recipe and Edit to update your own recipe. Only Owner can edit and delete their recipes.
  • Use Check to read recipe. If you have a long ingredients list and many steps in instruction, click and scroll down to see full of the content.
  • Use CheckNutrient to check for calories, carb and fat.
  • Use Calculator to calculate the calories per day based on your goal. No data is saved here so only you who can check and see them.
  • We handle your personal data (email, password and recipes) according to Swedish Personal Data Act (1998: 204) PUL so contact admin tt222kj@lnu.student.se whenever you don't want your account anymore.
